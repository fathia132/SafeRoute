Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

SafeRoute collects the following information to provide our safety services:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number)
  • Real-time GPS location data
  • Emergency contact information
  • Device information for crash logs
  • Usage analytics (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for:

  • Sending emergency alerts to your contacts
  • Sharing your location during emergencies
  • Contacting authorities when needed
  • Improving app security and performance
  • Providing customer support

3. Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for location data
  • Password hashing using secure algorithms
  • HTTPS only for all connections
  • Regular security audits
  • No data sharing with third parties without consent

4. Location Tracking

Location data is only shared during an emergency. You control who receives this information through your emergency contacts list. Location history is not stored permanently.

5. Third-Party Services

SafeRoute uses Twilio for SMS and calling services. Twilio's privacy policy applies to data processed through their service. We do not use analytics or advertising trackers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Update your information anytime
  • Withdraw consent for data collection
  • Request data portability

7. Data Retention

We retain your account information as long as your account is active. Emergency alert logs are kept for 90 days for safety purposes. You can request deletion anytime.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. You will be notified of any major changes through the app or email.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or requests, contact us at: privacy@saferoute.app
