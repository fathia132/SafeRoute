Your Privacy Matters

Safety Route is built to protect you.
Here is exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: June 2025
👋
Who We Are

SafetyRoute is a women's safety app that helps you travel with confidence. We use journey timers, reminders, and emergency alerts to keep you and your loved ones informed when it matters most.

We take your privacy seriously. We only collect what we absolutely need to keep you safe — nothing more.

📋
Data We Collect

When you create an account or use Safety Saver, we may collect:

  • Your name and email address when you sign up
  • Your emergency contact phone number
  • Destinations you save for your journeys
  • Journey history — start time, duration, and outcome (safe / extended / emergency)
  • Reminder preferences — time, day, and destination
  • Login method — email, Google, or Facebook

✅ We do NOT collect your real-time GPS coordinates. Destination names are text you type yourself — we never track your live location.

🚨
Emergency Contact Number

You may choose to save an emergency contact phone number in the app. This number is stored securely in our database and is only used for one purpose:

  • To make an automated phone call via Twilio if your journey timer expires and you do not press "I'm Safe" within 20 seconds

The call will play a voice message informing your contact that you may need assistance and where you were heading.

⚠️ Your emergency contact number is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than your safety alerts.

📍
Location Use

Safety Saver does not request or store your device's GPS location. We do not run background location tracking.

The only "location" data we hold is the destination text you manually type when setting a reminder or starting a journey — for example, "Yaba Market" or "Lagos Island." This text is stored solely to include in your journey history and emergency call message.

✅ You are always in control. You type the destination. We never ask your phone for coordinates.

⚙️
How We Use Your Data
  • To create and manage your account securely
  • To run your journey timer and send you safety reminders
  • To call your emergency contact if you do not check in on time
  • To show you your journey history
  • To improve the app experience over time

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your safety.

🔗
Third-Party Services

Safety Route uses a small number of trusted third-party services to function:

  • Google OAuth — if you choose to sign in with Google, Google authenticates you. We only receive your name and email.
  • Facebook OAuth — same as Google. We only receive your name and email.
  • Twilio — used to make the emergency phone call to your saved contact. Twilio only receives the phone number and the call message at the moment the alert is triggered.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

✊
Your Rights
  • You can update or delete your emergency contact at any time from the Saved page
  • You can delete your journey history at any time
  • You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • You can stop using the app at any time — we do not lock you in
🔒
Security

Your password is hashed using industry-standard encryption before being stored — we never store it in plain text. Your data is stored in a secured database and is only accessible to your own account.

While no system is 100% immune to breaches, we take reasonable and responsible steps to protect your information.
